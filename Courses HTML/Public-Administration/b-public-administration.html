BACHELOR OF PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION

A four-year degree program in the Philippines is designed to prepare you for a career in public service. Enrolling in this program will help you become more familiar with the different government agencies, their interrelationships, the individuals managing them, the policies they enact, and their implementation. Additionally, it will aid in understanding the effective management of public resources, including budget preparation, expenditure control, administration, and evaluation of government programs, as well as ethical decision-making.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Microeconomics
  • Macroeconomics
  • Sociology/Psychology
  • Business Math
  • Basic Accounting
  • Elementary Statistics
  • Philippine Government and Constitution
  • Introduction to Public Administration
  • Philippine Administrative Thought and Institutions
  • Ethics and Accountability in the Public Service
