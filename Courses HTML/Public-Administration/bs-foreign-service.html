BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN FOREIGN SERVICE

A four-year degree program that aims to train individuals in maintaining peaceful relations with other countries, facilitating international investment flows, promoting tourism, and addressing the needs of Filipinos working abroad, among other objectives.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Protocol & Etiquette
  • Diplomatic and Consular Practices and Procedures
  • Cross Cultural Communication
  • Philippine Foreign Policy
  • International Organizations
  • International Trade Practices and Trends
  • Import-Export Procedures
  • Philippine Political and Socio-Cultural Conditions
  • World History
  • Foreign Language Elective (Japanese, French, Spanish, Mandarin)
