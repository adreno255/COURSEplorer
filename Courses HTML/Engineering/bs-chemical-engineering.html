BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN CHEMICAL ENGINEERING

A five-year degree program in the Philippines focuses on applying various sciences, including natural, social, formal, and applied sciences, to develop safe, ethical, and economical methods for transforming raw materials into more valuable products.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Solid Mensuration
  • Differential Equations
  • Differential Calculus
  • Integral Calculus
  • Statics of Rigid Bodies
  • Mechanics of Deformable Bodies
  • Industrial Chemistry
  • Chemical Engineering Calculations
  • Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics in Chemical Engineering
