BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN MINING ENGINEERING

A five-year degree program in the Philippines focuses on mines and mining. It encompasses activities such as evaluating the mining potential of land or areas, designing and constructing mines, overseeing their operations, and ensuring compliance with environmental laws and regulations, among other responsibilities.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Principles of Mining
  • Basic Mineralogy
  • Underground Mining
  • Surface Mining
  • Techniques of Mining Analysis
  • Petrology
  • Rock Mechanics
  • Mineral Processing
  • Mine Design
  • Mine Management
