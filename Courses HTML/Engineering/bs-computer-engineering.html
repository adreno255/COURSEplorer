BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN COMPUTER ENGINEERING

A five-year degree program in the Philippines focuses on training students in the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems, encompassing both hardware and software aspects. Alongside technical training, the program also provides knowledge of business principles and practices, preparing graduates for potential corporate careers post-graduation.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Computer Engineering Drafting and Design
  • Control Systems
  • Computer Systems Architecture
  • Computer System Organization with Assembly Language
  • Data Communications
  • Computer Networks
  • Data Structures and Algorithms Analysis
  • Operating Systems
  • System Analysis and Design
  • Engineering Ethics and Computer Laws
