BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INDUSTRIAL ENGINEERING

The five-year degree program in the Philippines focuses on the design, operation, management, and maintenance of various systems, processes, and facilities to optimize their efficiency. It offers three main fields of specialization: Production Engineering, Organization and Decision Systems, and Ergonomics/Human Factors Engineering.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Engineering Drawing
  • Computer Aided Drafting
  • Computer Fundamentals and Programming
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Principles of Economics
  • Managerial Accounting
  • Advanced Mathematics for Industrial Engineering
  • Industrial Materials and Processes
  • Industrial Quality Control
  • Facilities Planning and Design
