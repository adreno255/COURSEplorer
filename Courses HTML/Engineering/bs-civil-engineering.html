BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN CIVIL ENGINEERING

A five-year degree program in the Philippines focuses on using scientific and mathematical principles to design and construct buildings and infrastructure, including roads, bridges, tunnels, dams, and airports.

By enrolling in this program, you will learn the essential considerations for constructing buildings and infrastructure. These include understanding the natural contour of the land, the chemical properties of the soil, the types of materials to use, and how these factors affect the structure's ability to withstand natural and man-made disasters.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Surveying
  • Building Design
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics for Civil Engineering
  • Structural Theory
  • Structural Design
  • Hydraulics
  • Hydrology
