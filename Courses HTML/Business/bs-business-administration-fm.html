BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (MAJOR IN FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT)

It is a four-year business program in the Philippines specializing in the financial management of businesses.

You'll explore topics such as how financial institutions generate profits, the processes behind setting prices for goods and services, effective management of assets and liabilities, and the impact of government regulations on businesses.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Credit and Collection
  • Basic Microeconomics
  • Accounting II
  • Business Law (Obligation and Contract)
  • Total Quality Management
  • Capital Markets
  • Strategic Financial Management
  • Banking and Financial Institutions
  • Financial Analysis and Reporting
  • Good Governance and Social Responsibility
