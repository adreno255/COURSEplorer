BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (MAJOR IN HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT)

A four-year business program in the Philippines is designed to equip you for a career in Human Resource Development (HRDM) across diverse organizations and businesses.

In HRDM, which is a branch of Business Administration, you'll learn how to effectively manage your workforce. This involves tasks such as recruiting the best candidates, preparing them for their roles, and helping them acclimate to the workplace environment.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Labor Law and Legislation
  • Basic Microeconomics
  • Accounting
  • Business Law (Obligation and Contract)
  • Total Quality Management
  • Organizational Development
  • Recruitment and Selection
  • Labor Relations and Negotiations
  • Compensation Administration
  • Good Governance and Social Responsibility
