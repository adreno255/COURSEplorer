BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (MAJOR IN MARKETING MANAGEMENT)

The (BSBA-Marketing Management) program, a four-year business course in the Philippines, is designed to prepare you for a career in Marketing across diverse organizations and businesses.

In this program, you'll discover how to recognize business prospects, evaluate their advantages and drawbacks, and develop strategies to increase profits while mitigating potential losses. Additionally, you'll learn how to develop, launch, and market products using a range of tools and techniques, from traditional to cutting-edge methods.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Professional Salesmanship
  • Marketing Management
  • Distribution Management
  • Advertising and Sales Promo
  • Product Management
  • Retail Management
  • Strategic Financial Management
  • Consumer Behavior
  • Sales Management
  • International Marketing
