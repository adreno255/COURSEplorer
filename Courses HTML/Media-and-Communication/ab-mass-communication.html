BACHELOR OF ARTS IN MASS COMMUNICATION

The Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication (AB Mass Comm) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines designed to equip students with the skills to effectively utilize various media platforms for delivering specific messages to targeted audiences.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Intro to Communication Theory
  • Introduction to Journalism
  • Introduction to Broadcasting
  • Communication and Society
  • Developmental Communication
  • Desktop Publishing
  • Radio/TV Production
  • Mass Communication Research
  • Photojournalism
  • Mass Media Laws and Ethics
