BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN AGRIBUSINESS

The Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness (BSAB) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that focuses on training students in the manufacturing and distribution of farm supplies and related products, aligning with the country's bio-physical, socio-cultural, political, economic, and development needs.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Fundamentals of Crop Science
  • Fundamentals of Horticulture
  • Entomology
  • Plant Pathology
  • Introduction to Animal Science
