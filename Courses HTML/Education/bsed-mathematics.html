BACHELOR IN SECONDARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN MATHEMATICS)

It is a four-year degree program in the Philippines aimed at preparing you to teach various branches of Mathematics, including algebra, geometry, trigonometry, calculus, and statistics, in high school.

As a Mathematics major, you'll cover the same professional education courses outlined in the Bachelor of Secondary Education program, along with a few additional subjects tailored to Mathematics instruction.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • History of Math
  • Action Research in Mathematics Education
  • Mathematical Investigations and Modeling
  • Seminar on Technology in Mathematics
