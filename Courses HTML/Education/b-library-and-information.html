BACHELOR IN LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SCIENCE

This is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that educates individuals about various sources of information, how to obtain them, and how to build and manage a collection of reference materials. It also covers the management of both small and large-scale libraries, including handling library facilities, equipment, finances, and personnel.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Introduction to Library and Information Science
  • Collection Management
  • Organization of Information Sources
  • Information Sources and Services
  • Library and Information Management
  • Indexing and Abstracting
  • Information Technology
  • Research Methods in Library and Information Science
  • Special Materials
  • Library Literature for Children and Young Adults
