BACHELOR IN SECONDARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN VALUES EDUCATION)

This four-year degree program in the Philippines is designed to ready you for teaching Values Education subjects in high school. It covers the philosophical, ethical, socio-cultural, psychological, and legal aspects of values education. By choosing this major, you'll learn about the psycho-spiritual development of individuals, Filipino values, and how to effectively communicate with others.

You'll enroll in the same professional education courses outlined in the Bachelor of Secondary Education program, along with a few additional subjects.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Personhood Development
  • Psychological Theories of Values Development
  • Career Development and Work Values
  • Teaching Approaches and Strategies in Transformative Education
  • Values Integration in the School System
  • Behavioral Statistics
