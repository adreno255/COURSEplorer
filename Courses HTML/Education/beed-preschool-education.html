BACHELOR IN ELEMENTARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN PRESCHOOL EDUCATION)

This is a four-year degree program in the Philippines designed to prepare individuals for teaching preschool children.

As a Preschool Education major, you'll cover the same education courses found in the general BEEd program. However, rather than the content courses in BEEd, you'll focus on specialization courses tailored to teaching preschoolers. These courses go beyond basic subjects like reading and writing, incorporating social interaction into the learning process.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Preschool Curriculum
  • Observational Child Study
  • Organization and Management of Child Development Programs
  • Principles of Teaching
  • Child and Adolescent Development
  • Facilitating Learning
  • Social Dimensions of Education
  • The Teaching Professions
  • Principles of Teaching
  • Assessment of Student Learning
