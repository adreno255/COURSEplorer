BACHELOR IN SECONDARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN PHYSICAL SCIENCES)

It is a comprehensive four-year degree program in the Philippines specifically crafted to provide you with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in teaching Physical Science subjects at the high school level. Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical experiences, this program prepares you to effectively communicate complex scientific concepts.

As a Physical Sciences major, you'll take the same professional education courses as those in the Bachelor of Secondary Education program. Additionally, you'll have lectures and lab sessions designed specifically for teaching Physical Sciences.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Electricity and Magnetism
  • Thermodynamics
  • Basic Electronics
  • Astronomy
  • Biochemistry
  • Mechanics
  • Science, Technology and Society
