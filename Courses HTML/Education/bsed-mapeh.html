BACHELOR IN SECONDARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN MUSIC, ARTS, PHYSICAL AND HEALTH EDUCATION)

It is a four-year degree program in the Philippines focused on preparing you to teach Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health subjects in high school. The program covers a wide range of MAPEH activities, including Philippine and Asian music, local and international folk dances, individual and team sports, as well as coaching and officiating sports events, dance competitions, and music activities.

As part of this program, you'll study the same professional education courses outlined in the Bachelor of Secondary Education program, along with additional subjects tailored to Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health instruction.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Foundations of MAPEH
  • Personal, Community, Environmental Health, Safety Education and First Aid
  • Anatomical, Mechanical and Physiological Bases of Movement
  • Methods and Strategies in Teaching MAPEH
  • Special Education for MAPEH
