BACHELOR IN SECONDARY EDUCATION (MAJOR IN BIOLOGICAL SCIENCES)

It is a four-year degree program in the Philippines designed to equip you for teaching Biological Science subjects in high school. Alongside the professional education courses outlined in the Bachelor of Secondary Education program, you'll also engage in lectures and laboratory work specific to Biological Science.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Biological Sciences
  • Earth and Environmental Science
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Cell Biology
  • Ecology
  • Microbiology
  • Genetics
  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Biotechniques
  • Physics for Health Sciences
