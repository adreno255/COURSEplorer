BACHELOR OF ARTS IN LITERATURE

The Bachelor of Arts in Literature (AB Lit) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that explores the various literatures of the world, encompassing their history, forms, structures, modes, and techniques. It equips you with the skills to produce new literary works or critically analyze existing ones.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Literary Theory and Criticism
  • Literary and Cultural Studies
  • Creative Writing
  • World Literature (Asian, American, European)
  • Philippine Literature
