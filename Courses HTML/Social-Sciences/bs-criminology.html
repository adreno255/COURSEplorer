BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN CRIMINOLOGY

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BS Crim) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines focused on understanding the nature of crimes and criminals. This program will help you explore what crime is, how crimes occur, and the factors that make individuals more likely to commit crimes or become victims.

Additionally, you will learn about the workings of the justice system, the proper handling of alleged and convicted criminals, and the methods used for their rehabilitation and reform.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Introduction to Criminology and Psychology of Crimes
  • Philippine Criminal Justice System
  • Ethics and Values
  • Juvenile Delinquency and Crime Prevention
  • Human Behavior and Crisis Management
