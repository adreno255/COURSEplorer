BACHELOR OF ARTS IN ECONOMICS

The Bachelor of Arts in Economics (AB Economics) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of economic systems and their structures. This includes examining the entities that compose these systems, their interrelationships, and the impact of external factors on the entire system.

The program covers topics such as economic production, the consumption of goods and services, wealth distribution, labor, power relations, and taxation, among other areas.
