BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN PHYSICAL THERAPY

The Bachelor of Science in Physical Therapy (BSPT) is a five-year degree program in the Philippines focused on using treatments and exercises to restore function in injured bones or compensate for the loss of certain body parts.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Kinesiology
  • Psychiatric Foundations for Physical Therapy
  • Physical Therapy Examination and Evaluation
  • Therapeutic Exercises
