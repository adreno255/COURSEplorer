BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN NURSING

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines centered around caring for the sick or injured. This involves addressing immediate health threats and guiding patients through physical, mental, and emotional recovery.

As a nursing student, you will learn to conduct health assessments, provide preoperative, intraoperative, and postoperative care, perform life-saving interventions, respond to emergencies, and maintain medical records. Additionally, you will learn about the administrative aspects of nursing, including hospital organizational structures, patient classification systems, task delegation, staffing, scheduling, and performance evaluation.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Care of Mother, Child, and Family
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Microbiology and Parasitology
  • Bioethics
  • Care of Mother, Child, Family, and Population Group at Risk or with Problems
