BACHELOR OF FINE ARTS
(MAJOR IN VISUAL COMMUNICATION)

A four-year degree program in the Philippines focused on utilizing both traditional and modern technology to craft compelling advertisements, web designs, and designs for new media platforms.

In this major, you'll explore various types of lettering and their structures, along with aspects like word and line spacing, letter sizes and forms, layout formats, and their utilization in producing publications such as books, magazines, and pamphlets. Additionally, you'll delve into design concepts, the factors shaping them, and current trends in the Philippine design industry.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Figure Drawing
  • Visual Design
  • Visual Verbal Communication
  • Advertising Design
  • Production Methods
  • Design Workshop
  • Photography
  • Production for Electronic Media
  • Design Theory
