BACHELOR OF ARTS IN PHILOSOPHY

A four-year degree program in the Philippines primarily focuses on exploring the fundamental principles of reality, knowledge, morality, and existence, and their applications across diverse fields.

PROGRAM EXPECTATIONS

Below are examples of courses that you may take in this program

  • Ancient philosophy
  • Medieval Philosophy
  • Modern philosophy
  • Epistemology
  • Political Philosophy
  • Metaphysics
  • Ethics
  • Philosophy of Person
  • Philosophy of History
  • Filipino Philosophy
