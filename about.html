OBJECTIVES

To develop a system that showcases available college bachelor's degree programs and provides comprehensive guidance to incoming freshmen students, assisting them in making informed decisions about their prospective courses and career paths.

SCOPE

The project aims to display available college bachelor degrees across various courses, informing students about course expectations and offering insights into the student experience. It will also enumerate potential career and job opportunities for each course, helping students make informed decisions about their future.
